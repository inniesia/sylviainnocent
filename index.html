I'm Sylvia Innocent — equally at home developing construction documents as I am crafting a digital illustration. My practice lives at the edge of two disciplines, and I wouldn't have it any other way.

My MArch from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago shaped a deeply interdisciplinary approach. Currently at FitzGerald, working across architecture and interior design. I believe the best spaces tell stories — and so does the best art.
